Deverakonda Vijay Sai (born 9 May 1989), widely known as Vijay Deverakonda,[1] is an Indian actor and film producer who works in Telugu films.[2][3] Vijay is an recipient of a Filmfare Award, a Nandi Award and three SIIMA Awards.[4] Deverakonda made his debut with Nuvvila (2011) and received praise for his supporting role in the coming-of-age drama Yevade Subramanyam (2015). He rose to prominence as a leading film actor by starring in Pelli Choopulu (2016) and Arjun Reddy (2017), winning the Filmfare Award for Best Actor â€“ Telugu for his performance as a volatile surgeon in the latter. He further established himself with commercially successful films such as Mahanati (2018), Geetha Govindam (2018), and Taxiwaala (2018).[5][6] This success was followed by a series of poorly received films. In 2019, Deverakonda launched his own production house King of the Hill Entertainment, which produced Meeku Maathrame Cheptha (2019) and Pushpaka Vimanam (2021).[7][8] Beyond his film career, Deverakonda has designed his own fashion brand Rowdy Wear, which launched on Myntra in 2020,[9] and is the co-owner of the volleyball team Hyderabad Black Hawks.[10] Deverakonda is also a celebrity endorser for numerous products and supports various humanitarian causes.

about Director

Prashanth Neel (born Prashanth Neelakantapuram on June 4, 1980) is an acclaimed Indian film director and screenwriter known for high-octane, dark, and gritty action thrillers. He primarily works in Kannada and Telugu cinema and gained national fame for directing the blockbuster K.G.F. franchise and the pan-Indian hit Salaar. Neel's films are characterized by their intense action sequences, stylized cinematography, and complex narratives. He has a knack for creating larger-than-life characters and gripping storylines that resonate with audiences across India. Prashanth Neel's work has significantly influenced contemporary Indian cinema, and he continues to be a prominent figure in the industry.

about music director


Ravi Basrur is an Indian music composer and sound designer known for his work in Kannada cinema. He gained widespread recognition for his powerful and evocative musical scores, particularly for the K.G.F. film series directed by Prashanth Neel. Basrur's compositions are characterized by their intense energy, blending traditional Indian musical elements with contemporary sounds to create a unique auditory experience that enhances the cinematic narrative. His work on K.G.F. was critically acclaimed and played a significant role in the films' success, earning him a reputation as one of the leading music directors in the Indian film industry. Ravi Basrur continues to contribute to various film projects, showcasing his versatility and creativity in music composition.

about awards
